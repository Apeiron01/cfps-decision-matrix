# PRISMA notes

## Screening log fields (CSV recommendation)
- record_id
- source (pubmed, scholar, other)
- query_id
- title
- authors
- year
- doi
- url
- decision (include/exclude/unsure)
- exclusion_reason
- reviewer
- notes

## PRISMA flow diagram counts
- records_identified
- duplicates_removed
- records_screened
- records_excluded
- full_text_assessed
- full_text_excluded
- studies_included

Use these counts consistently across the screening log and the final PRISMA diagram.
